What civil engineering services do you offer?

I offer a comprehensive range of civil engineering services including structural design, construction project management, AutoCAD & Civil 3D drafting, site planning, road & drainage design, and building inspection. Whether it's a residential, commercial, or infrastructure project, I provide expert engineering solutions from concept to completion.

How do I get started with a project?

Simply reach out via the Contact page or email me at contact@elitechwiz.com / call +255 688 164 510. We will schedule an initial consultation to discuss your project requirements, timeline, and budget before proceeding to detailed design and planning.

Do you handle both residential and commercial projects?

Yes. I have experience designing and managing a wide variety of projects including residential houses, apartment blocks, commercial buildings, bridges, roads, and water supply infrastructure.

What software tools do you use?

I use industry-standard tools including AutoCAD, Civil 3D, SAP2000, STAAD.Pro, ETABS, and Revit for structural and civil designs, as well as Microsoft Project for construction scheduling and project management.

Can you provide structural calculations and drawings?

Absolutely. I prepare complete structural analysis reports, foundation designs, reinforced concrete designs (beams, columns, slabs), and full working drawings in compliance with applicable building codes and standards.

Do you provide site supervision and inspection services?

Yes. I offer on-site supervision and quality inspection services to ensure construction work is executed according to design specifications and safety standards, minimising costly errors and rework.

How long does a typical structural design take?

Timelines depend on the project scope. A typical residential structural design takes 1–2 weeks, while larger commercial or infrastructure projects may take 4–8 weeks. I always provide a clear project schedule at the start so deadlines are transparent.

What are your rates?

Rates vary depending on project complexity, size, and location. Please visit the Pricing page for package details or contact me directly for a custom quote tailored to your project.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes. I work with clients both locally and internationally. For remote projects, I deliver all designs, drawings, and reports digitally in PDF, DWG, or other required formats, and hold regular video consultations to keep you informed and involved.

How do I contact you for a quote?

You can reach me by email at contact@elitechwiz.com, call/WhatsApp at +255 688 164 510 or +255 742 631 101, or simply use the Contact form on this site. I respond to all enquiries within 24 hours.
